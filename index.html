Mapping the Collective Reddit Brain

Technologies: Python, Network Analysis, Data Visualization

We treated Reddit as a nervous system — subreddits as neurons, hyperlinks as action potentials, and linguistic co-activation as functional connectivity — asking whether a platform of millions behaves like a brain.

  • Interaction networks — Built a structural connectome from hundreds of thousands of hyperlinks, classifying communities into sensory, interneuron, and motor archetypes by in/out-degree, with 300-dimensional embeddings as "axon length" proxies.
  • NLP & feature extraction — Compressed LIWC psychological signals into six coherent neural signals per subreddit (affect, cognition, social orientation, etc.) to capture meaningful dynamics across time.
  • Functional vs. structural connectivity — Isolated subreddits that co-evolve without ever directly linking, analogous to fMRI connectivity beyond anatomical wiring.
  • Visualizations — Interactive dashboards for sentiment flow, hub resilience, and a 3D brain projection mapping subreddit categories onto MNI brain regions.
